About Frans

With over 100 million streams to his name, Frans is one of Sweden's most recognized voices.

His career began at the age of 7 with a breakout hit that topped the national charts. Since then, he's performed on national television across several countries, won Melodifestivalen, and represented Sweden at Eurovision, finishing in the top 5.

Today, Frans continues to evolve — writing, recording, and performing with a focus on raw energy, live instrumentation, and emotional connection.

DISCOGRAPHY

Frans is a storyteller at heart, blending soulful melodies with a raw, rhythmic edge. Together with his band, he crafts a sound that's both intimate and bold — a genre-bending mix he calls Jeaggepop.

MUSICAL STORYTELLING

Frans believes in the power of music to tell stories that connect hearts and minds. His Jeaggepop sound bridges genres, creating a unique musical language that speaks to diverse audiences.

Through intimate melodies and bold rhythmic arrangements, Frans crafts songs that are both personal and universal, inviting listeners into a world where Jazz, Reggae, and Pop converge.

"Music is the universal language of storytelling"
- Frans, 2024
